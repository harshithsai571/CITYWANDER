🧳 CityWander Travel Tips

📌 General Instructions

  • Always carry a government-issued ID.
  • Research local customs and respect them.
  • Pre-book accommodation wherever possible.
  • Keep digital and printed copies of travel documents.

🛡️ Safety Measures

  • Do not share personal information with strangers.
  • Use registered transportation services only.
  • Stay in well-lit and populated areas at night.
  • Keep emergency contacts accessible.

✈️ Travel Tips

  • Travel light and smart – carry only essentials.
  • Stay hydrated and eat local food cautiously.
  • Learn a few local phrases – it helps!
  • Have travel insurance for unexpected situations.
